# KDP Listing Scoring System

## Overview

This scoring system provides a comprehensive analysis of Amazon KDP book listings, similar to professional optimization tools. Use this framework to evaluate and improve listings.

---

## Master Scorecard (100 Points Total)

| Category | Max Points | Weight |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Title & Subtitle | 20 | 20% |
| Book Description | 25 | 25% |
| Backend Keywords | 15 | 15% |
| Categories | 15 | 15% |
| Cover Design | 15 | 15% |
| Social Proof | 10 | 10% |
| **TOTAL** | **100** | 100% |

---

## Category 1: Title & Subtitle (20 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Primary keyword in title | 0-4 | 4=present, 2=partial, 0=missing |
| First 80 chars optimized (mobile) | 0-3 | 3=key info visible, 0=cut off |
| Natural readability | 0-3 | 3=flows well, 0=keyword stuffed |
| Subtitle uses additional keywords | 0-3 | 3=strategic, 0=wasted |
| Compelling/intriguing | 0-3 | 3=creates curiosity, 0=bland |
| Appropriate length | 0-2 | 2=optimal, 0=too long/short |
| No prohibited characters | 0-2 | 2=clean, 0=has issues |

### Score Interpretation:
- **17-20:** Excellent - Title is highly optimized
- **13-16:** Good - Minor improvements possible
- **9-12:** Fair - Needs optimization
- **0-8:** Poor - Major revision needed

---

## Category 2: Book Description (25 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Strong hook in first 200 chars | 0-5 | 5=compelling, 0=weak/missing |
| Keywords naturally integrated | 0-4 | 4=seamless, 0=stuffed or absent |
| HTML formatting used | 0-3 | 3=well-formatted, 0=plain text |
| Clear value proposition | 0-3 | 3=clear benefits, 0=unclear |
| Bullet points/lists | 0-3 | 3=scannable, 0=wall of text |
| Target audience mentioned | 0-2 | 2=clear "who is this for", 0=vague |
| Call-to-action present | 0-2 | 2=strong CTA, 0=missing |
| Emotional triggers | 0-2 | 2=engages emotions, 0=dry |
| Character count utilization | 0-1 | 1=uses available space, 0=too short |

### Score Interpretation:
- **21-25:** Excellent - Description sells the book
- **16-20:** Good - Solid foundation
- **11-15:** Fair - Missing key elements
- **0-10:** Poor - Complete rewrite needed

---

## Category 3: Backend Keywords (15 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| All 7 boxes utilized | 0-3 | 3=all used, 2=5-6, 1=3-4, 0=less |
| No title word repetition | 0-2 | 2=no repeats, 0=duplicates |
| Mix of phrase lengths | 0-2 | 2=strategic mix, 0=random |
| Relevant to content | 0-2 | 2=all relevant, 0=keyword stuffing |
| Long-tail keywords included | 0-2 | 2=present, 0=only broad terms |
| Audience identifiers | 0-2 | 2=clear targeting, 0=missing |
| Competitor/comparison terms | 0-2 | 2=strategic comparisons, 0=none |

### Score Interpretation:
- **13-15:** Excellent - Keywords fully optimized
- **10-12:** Good - Strong keyword strategy
- **6-9:** Fair - Missing opportunities
- **0-5:** Poor - Major keyword work needed

---

## Category 4: Categories (15 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| All 3 categories used | 0-3 | 3=all used, 2=2 used, 0-1=fewer |
| Categories relevant to content | 0-4 | 4=all relevant, 0=mismatch |
| Strategic tier mix (broad/niche) | 0-3 | 3=good mix, 0=all same level |
| Bestseller potential in at least 1 | 0-3 | 3=achievable ranking, 0=impossible |
| No ghost categories | 0-2 | 2=all award badges, 0=ghost cats |

### Score Interpretation:
- **13-15:** Excellent - Perfect category strategy
- **10-12:** Good - Well positioned
- **6-9:** Fair - Suboptimal placement
- **0-5:** Poor - Wrong categories

---

## Category 5: Cover Design (15 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Genre-appropriate | 0-4 | 4=clearly signals genre, 0=confusing |
| Thumbnail readability | 0-3 | 3=clear at small size, 0=illegible |
| Professional quality | 0-3 | 3=professional, 0=amateur |
| Title visible & readable | 0-2 | 2=easy to read, 0=hard to see |
| Color contrast | 0-2 | 2=high contrast, 0=muddy |
| Unique/stands out | 0-1 | 1=distinctive, 0=generic |

### Score Interpretation:
- **13-15:** Excellent - Cover sells the book
- **10-12:** Good - Professional quality
- **6-9:** Fair - Needs improvement
- **0-5:** Poor - Consider redesign

---

## Category 6: Social Proof (10 Points)

### Scoring Criteria:

| Criteria | Points | How to Score |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Number of reviews | 0-4 | 4=50+, 3=20-49, 2=10-19, 1=1-9, 0=none |
| Average rating | 0-3 | 3=4.5+, 2=4.0-4.4, 1=3.5-3.9, 0=<3.5 |
| Recent reviews (last 30 days) | 0-2 | 2=recent activity, 0=stale |
| Review quality (detailed) | 0-1 | 1=detailed reviews, 0=sparse |

### Score Interpretation:
- **9-10:** Excellent - Strong social proof
- **6-8:** Good - Decent credibility
- **3-5:** Fair - Needs more reviews
- **0-2:** Poor - Critical to get reviews

---

## Overall Score Interpretation

| Total Score | Grade | Assessment |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| 90-100 | A+ | Exceptional - Top-tier listing |
| 80-89 | A | Excellent - Minor tweaks only |
| 70-79 | B | Good - Some optimization needed |
| 60-69 | C | Fair - Multiple areas need work |
| 50-59 | D | Poor - Significant issues |
| 0-49 | F | Failing - Major overhaul needed |
