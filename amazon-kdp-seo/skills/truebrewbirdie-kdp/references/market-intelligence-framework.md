# Market Intelligence Framework

Complete framework for analyzing Amazon KDP book markets - similar to professional tools like Listing Optimization AI.

## Analysis Components

| Component | Purpose | Data Points |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Review Analysis** | Understand customer sentiment | 50-200 reviews |
| **Customer Avatars** | Define target audiences | 3-5 personas |
| **Competitive Landscape** | Map market positioning | 10-15 competitors |
| **Pain Points** | Identify unmet needs | Top 10 issues |
| **Feature Requests** | Find product gaps | Top 10 requests |
| **Strategic Recommendations** | Actionable insights | 4 strategy areas |

## 5. Strategic Recommendations Framework

### Pricing Strategy

| Approach | When to Use | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Penetration** | New market entry | Launch at $4.99, raise to $9.99 |
| **Premium** | Quality differentiation | $14.99+ with thicker paper |
| **Competitive** | Mature market | Match top competitor |
| **Bundle** | Multiple products | 3-book set discount |

### Product Strategy

| Strategy | Description | Investment |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Quality Upgrade** | Address paper/print issues | Medium |
| **Design Expansion** | More variety in themes | Low |
| **Format Variation** | Large print, spiral | High |
| **Series Development** | Related themed books | Medium |

### Marketing Strategy

| Channel | Target Avatar | Message Focus |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| Instagram | Mindful Artist | Process videos, finished art |
| Pinterest | Home Decorator | Room setups, gift ideas |
| TikTok | Creative Teen | ASMR coloring, trends |
| Facebook | Senior Hobbyist | Community, ease of use |

### Operations Strategy

| Area | Recommendation | Impact |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **Print Quality** | Use premium paper option | Reduces negative reviews |
| **Inventory** | POD for custom variations | Low risk testing |
| **Suppliers** | Compare KDP vs IngramSpark | May improve quality |

## 6. Analysis Checklist

### Before Analysis
- [ ] Identify main product ASIN
- [ ] Find 10-15 competitors in same category
- [ ] Collect 50-200 reviews across products
- [ ] Note price points and formats

### During Analysis
- [ ] Categorize review sentiment
- [ ] Extract common themes (positive/negative)
- [ ] Identify customer demographics from review language
- [ ] Map competitive positioning
- [ ] Calculate estimated market shares

### After Analysis
- [ ] Define 3-5 customer avatars
- [ ] Prioritize pain points by impact
- [ ] Generate strategic recommendations
- [ ] Create actionable implementation plan

## 8. Tools for Data Collection

### Free Methods
- **Amazon Reviews**: Read top and critical reviews
- **Look Inside**: Analyze competitor content
- **BSR History**: Use Keepa browser extension
- **Autocomplete**: Amazon search suggestions

### Paid Tools
- **Listing Optimization AI**: Full market intelligence
- **Publisher Rocket**: KDP-specific keywords/categories
- **Helium 10**: General Amazon analytics
- **Jungle Scout**: Competition tracking

### Manual Review Mining
1. Go to competitor's listing
2. Filter reviews by star rating
3. Read 20 most helpful reviews (each rating)
4. Extract themes into spreadsheet
5. Count frequency of mentions
