# /awareness-check

Identify where your audience sits on the awareness spectrum and get positioning recommendations.

## Instructions

1. Gather information about:
   - The product/service
   - The target audience
   - Current marketing approach

2. Load copythinking-awareness skill
3. Analyze against the 5 awareness levels:
   - Unaware
   - Problem-aware
   - Solution-aware
   - Product-aware
   - Most-aware

4. Provide specific messaging recommendations for their level
5. Suggest progression strategies
