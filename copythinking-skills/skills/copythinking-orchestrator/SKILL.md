## ADHD-Friendly Output Rules

When orchestrating skills for ADHD users:
- **Break into chunks** - Never overwhelm with walls of text
- **Use visual hierarchy** - Bold, bullets, spacing
- **Action-first** - Lead with what to DO
- **Examples over theory** - Show, don't just tell
- **One concept at a time** - Sequential, not parallel

## Quick Reference Tables

### Sophistication Level Quick Reference
When market sophistication comes up:
- **Level 1-2:** New market, simple claims work
- **Level 3-4:** Need mechanism (the "how")
- **Level 5:** Indirect approach, stories

### Awareness Level Quick Reference
- **Unaware:** Indirect, story-based
- **Problem-aware:** Agitate problem
- **Solution-aware:** Compare solutions
- **Product-aware:** Benefits & proof
- **Most-aware:** Deal & urgency

## Emergency Fallbacks

If unsure which skill to load:

## Key Principles Across All Skills

- Clarity > Cleverness
- Specificity > Vagueness
- Benefits > Features
- Emotion > Logic
- One reader > Everyone

## When NOT to Load Skills

Don't load copythinking skills for:
- Fiction writing
- Academic writing
- Technical documentation
- Poetry or creative writing
- SEO-only content
