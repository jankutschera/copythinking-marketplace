## VSL (Video Sales Letter) Script

### Structure (15-30 minutes)

```
[0-1 min] HOOK
- Pattern interrupt
- Big promise
- Qualify viewer

[1-5 min] PROBLEM AMPLIFICATION
- Identify their problem
- Show you understand
- Agitate the pain
- Reveal hidden costs

[5-10 min] YOUR STORY
- Your struggle (relatable)
- The breakthrough
- Your transformation

[10-15 min] THE SOLUTION
- Introduce your method/product
- Unique mechanism
- Why it's different

[15-20 min] PROOF
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Results

[20-25 min] THE OFFER
- What they get
- Bonus stack
- Value comparison

[25-28 min] RISK REVERSAL
- Guarantee explanation
- Why you can offer it

[28-30 min] CTA + URGENCY
- Clear next step
- What happens when they click
- Why act now
- Final push
```

## Quick Reference: Copy Length Guidelines

| Copy Type | Ideal Length |
|-----------|--------------|
| Tweet | 1-2 sentences |
| LinkedIn Post | 200-300 words |
| Email | 300-500 words |
| Landing Page | 500-1,000 words |
| Sales Page (low-ticket) | 1,500-3,000 words |
| Sales Page (high-ticket) | 3,000-8,000+ words |
| VSL | 15-45 minutes |
| Product Description | 150-300 words |
| About Page | 400-800 words |
