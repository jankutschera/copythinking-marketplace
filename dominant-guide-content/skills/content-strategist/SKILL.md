## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keyword Expansion

Start with seed keywords and expand using DataForSEO:

**Primary Seeds:**
- how to be a dom
- bdsm for beginners
- dominant submissive relationship
- bdsm training
- power dynamics relationship

**Category Seeds:**
- [category] + bdsm
- [category] + d/s relationship
- [category] + dominant guide

### Step 2: Keyword Qualification

Score keywords on these criteria:

| Criteria | Weight | Scoring |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **Search Volume** | 30% | >1000: High, 100-1000: Medium, <100: Low |
| **Competition** | 25% | KD <30: Easy, 30-60: Medium, >60: Hard |
| **Relevance** | 25% | Direct fit, Adjacent, Stretch |
| **Intent Match** | 20% | Informational, Commercial, Navigational |

### Step 3: Topic Clustering

Group keywords into topic clusters:

```
Cluster: "How to Be a Dom"
├── Pillar: Ultimate Guide to Being a Dom (3000+ words)
├── Supporting: How to Build Confidence as a Dom
├── Supporting: First-Time Dom Tips
├── Supporting: Common Dom Mistakes
├── Supporting: Finding Your Dominant Style
└── Related: Take the Dom Type Quiz
```

### Step 4: Content Gap Analysis

Compare our content against:

1. **Competitor content** - What do they have that we don't?
2. **SERP analysis** - What's ranking for our target keywords?
3. **PAA questions** - People Also Ask opportunities
4. **Related searches** - Additional angles

## Content Prioritization Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **P1 - Now** | High volume, low competition, high relevance | Create immediately |
| **P2 - Soon** | Medium volume, medium competition, high relevance | Plan for next month |
| **P3 - Later** | Lower volume OR higher competition, still relevant | Add to backlog |
| **P4 - Skip** | Low relevance or too competitive | Don't pursue |

## Monthly Content Calendar

Structure monthly output by content type:

| Week | Content Type | Focus |
|------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | Pillar Article | Major keyword, comprehensive |
| 2 | Supporting Article x2 | Cluster keywords |
| 3 | Update + New Article | Refresh old content + new |
| 4 | Supporting Article x2 | Complete cluster |

**Monthly Targets:**
- 1 pillar article (2500+ words)
- 4-6 supporting articles (1500+ words each)
- 2 content updates/refreshes
- 1 lead magnet or landing page
