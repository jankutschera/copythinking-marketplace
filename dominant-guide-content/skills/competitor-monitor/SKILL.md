## Monitoring Workflow

### 1. Content Discovery

Use web search to find recent content:

```
site:domsubliving.com new OR latest OR 2024 OR 2025
site:submissiveguide.com blog
site:badgirlsbible.com [topic keyword]
```

### 2. Keyword Tracking

Use DataForSEO/Sistrix to check:

1. **Ranked Keywords**
   - What keywords is competitor ranking for?
   - Any new keyword rankings in last 30 days?
   - Which keywords are we missing?

2. **Keyword Gaps**
   - Keywords competitor ranks for that we don't
   - High-volume opportunities
   - Low-competition wins

3. **SERP Features**
   - Does competitor have featured snippets?
   - FAQ schema implementations?
   - People Also Ask appearances?

### 3. Content Analysis

For each new competitor article found:

| Analysis Point | What to Check |
|----------------|---------------|
| **Topic** | Is this a gap in our content? |
| **Angle** | What unique perspective do they offer? |
| **Length** | Word count and depth |
| **Structure** | H2s, FAQs, internal links |
| **Keywords** | Primary and secondary targets |
| **CTAs** | What are they promoting? |

### 4. Lead Magnet Intelligence

Track competitor lead magnets:

| Competitor | Known Lead Magnets |
|------------|-------------------|
| DomSubLiving | Free training, webinar registration |
| SubmissiveGuide | Patreon tiers, community access |
| BadGirlsBible | Newsletter, product guides |
| SexualAlpha | Assessment quiz, free course |
| BDSMTrainingAcademy | Free intro course |

### 5. Social Media Monitoring

Check for new content signals:

- **YouTube:** New video titles and topics
- **Instagram:** Post themes and engagement
- **TikTok:** Trending content styles
- **Twitter/X:** Announcements and discussions
