## USAGE TIPS

### For Instagram Stories:
- Always use `portrait_16_9` for 9:16 vertical
- Leave 15% margins at top/bottom for text/stickers
- Direct flash style works great for attention

### For Instagram Feed:
- Square `square_hd` is safest for grid consistency
- Portrait `portrait_4_3` shows more of outfit/scene
- First carousel slide should be most eye-catching

### For Business Content:
- Text on whiteboards/screens must be readable
- Gesture toward key information for engagement
- Leave space for overlay text/logos

### For Product Shots:
- Clean backgrounds for e-commerce
- Lifestyle context for social media
- Shadow play for premium brands

### For Selfies:
- Smartphone perspective = slightly wide angle
- Elevated angle is most flattering
- Direct flash for Y2K/trendy aesthetic
