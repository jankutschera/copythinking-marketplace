# Nano Banana Pro - Technical Specifications Guide

A comprehensive reference for camera, lighting, and color grading parameters used in Nano Banana prompts.

---

## Camera & Lens Specifications

### Lens Focal Length Guide

| Focal Length | Use Case | Characteristics | Best For |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| **14-24mm** | Ultra-wide | Dramatic distortion, expansive | Architecture, landscapes, environmental |
| **24mm** | Wide | Some distortion, contextual | Street, documentary, environmental portraits |
| **35mm** | Moderate wide | Natural perspective with context | Documentary, street style, editorial |
| **50mm** | Standard | Most natural to human eye | Versatile, candid, natural portraits |
| **85mm** | Portrait | Flattering compression, beautiful bokeh | Headshots, beauty, fashion portraits |
| **135mm** | Telephoto | Strong compression, intimate feel | Compressed portraits, subject isolation |
| **200mm+** | Long telephoto | Extreme compression | Sports, wildlife, extreme subject isolation |

### Aperture Settings

| Aperture | Depth of Field | Light | Best For |
|----------|---------------|-------|----------|
| **f/1.2 - f/1.4** | Razor thin | Maximum | Dreamy portraits, extreme bokeh, low light |
| **f/1.8 - f/2.0** | Very shallow | Excellent | Standard portrait bokeh, subject isolation |
| **f/2.8** | Shallow | Good | Professional portrait standard, some context |
| **f/4** | Moderate | Moderate | Environmental portraits, balanced depth |
| **f/5.6 - f/8** | Deep | Less | Group shots, environmental, some landscape |
| **f/11 - f/16** | Very deep | Minimal | Landscape, architecture, everything sharp |

### Depth of Field Descriptions

Use these phrases in prompts:
- "Razor-thin depth of field with creamy bokeh"
- "Shallow depth of field with beautiful background blur"
- "Subject sharply isolated against soft bokeh"
- "Deep focus with entire scene sharp"
- "Balanced depth with environmental context visible"

## Combining Parameters

### Portrait Formula
```
Shot with [85mm/135mm] lens at f/[1.4-2.0], [Rembrandt/natural] lighting,
[warm/professional] color grading, shallow depth of field with [creamy/beautiful] bokeh
```

### Fashion Formula
```
Shot with [35mm/85mm] lens at f/[1.8-2.8], [studio/natural] lighting,
[high fashion/editorial] color grading, [sharp/artistic] focus
```

### Cinematic Formula
```
Shot with [35mm/50mm] lens at f/[2.0-2.8], [dramatic/cinematic] lighting,
[teal and orange/film] color grading, anamorphic [lens flares/bokeh]
```

### Documentary Formula
```
Shot with [35mm/50mm] lens at f/[2.0-4.0], [natural/available] lighting,
[natural/muted] color grading, [candid/authentic] feel
```
